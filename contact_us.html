Who are we?

We are a team from the University of Waterloo. We are all computer science majored and our goal is to promote the idea of gender equality.

What is W.E.?

W.E. is a website focused on helping woman. UN came up with the idea "Heforshe". But we think "heforshe" is not enough for woman equity. W.E. has two meaning. Firstly, it is short for "woman equity", which means our final goal is to fight for woman's equity. Also, W.E. stands for Weforshe in order to raise awareness from people disregard their gender and age. As everyone knows, UN comes up with the idea "Heforshe". But we think "heforshe" is not enough for woman equity. So we want men to respect woman's rights; want woman to fight for their equity.
